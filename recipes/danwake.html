Danwake

Description

Dan Wake: Mix bean flour, water, spices. Roll into balls, fry golden. Top with chopped veggies, egg, chili, or sauce. Savory Nigerian goodness!

Ingrediants

    Main:

  • 2 cups Danwake flour (pre-made or ground beans, cassava, baobab powder mix)
  • 1 -1.5 cups of water
  • i teaspoonn potash

    • Seasonings:

  • Mixed pepper
  • 1-2 Seasoning cubes
  • 1 medium onion, choped
  • Vegetable oil

    • Garnishes (optional):

  • Tomatoes, choped
  • Hard boiled abd sliced egg
  • Cocumber, sliced
  • Shredded or grated carrot
  • Ground cayenne pepper (jaji spice)

Steps

    Preparation:

  1. Make Danwake flour: If using pre-made, skip this step. Otherwise, follow your chosen recipe for making Danwake flour from scratch.
  2. Chop vegetables: Dice onion and prepare any desired garnishes like tomatoes, cucumber, carrots, etc

    3. Cooking the Danwake

  3. Mix dry ingredients: Combine Danwake flour, mixed pepper, and seasoning cubes in a bowl.
  4. Dissolve potash (optional): Mix potash in a small amount of water until dissolved (be cautious, it can irritate skin).
  5. Add water gradually: While mixing, slowly add water to the dry ingredients until you achieve a thick paste consistency (slightly wetter than dough but not runny).
  6. Heat oil: Add enough oil to a pot for shallow frying. Heat it over medium heat until shimmery.
  7. Form dumplings: Roll small portions of the paste into balls using your palms or a wet spoon.
  8. Drain and serve: Remove fried Danwake from oil and drain them on paper towels.

    9. Serving

  9. Garnish: Arrange the fried Danwake balls on a plate. Add your chosen garnishes on top or on the side for dipping.

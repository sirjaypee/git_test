Couscous Recipe

Discription

Jollof couscous: Spicy, vibrant twist on classic rice dish. Golden couscous infused with tomatoes, peppers, spices, and meat - Nigerian comfort food at its finest!

Ingrediants

    Main:

  • 2 cups couscous
  • 4 cups chicken or beef broth
  • 1 table spoon vegetable oil

    • Spices and Sesonings

  • 1 onion, chppoed
  • 2 cloves, minced
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, chpoed
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoom curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon paparika
  • Salt and black pepper

    • Vegetables

  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

    • Optional (protien):

  • 1 pound chicken thighs, skinless and boneless, chopped
  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cubed

Steps

    preparation:

  1. Chop vegetables: Dice the onion, garlic, bell peppers, and tomato. Chop the parsley. Prepare any protein you're using by cubing or chopping it.

    2. Cooking:

  2. Heat oil: Add the vegetable oil to a large pot or Dutch oven and heat it over medium heat.
  3. Saut√© aromatics: Once hot, add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Then, add the garlic and scotch bonnet pepper (if using) and cook for another minute.
  4. Add spices and herbs: Stir in the thyme and curry powder. Cook for 30 seconds to release their aroma
  5. Simmer broth and seasonings: Add the chicken or beef broth, bay leaves, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  6. Cook couscous: Once boiling, stir in the couscous and reduce heat to low. Cover the pot and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the couscous is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Fluff and rest: Remove the pot from heat and fluff the couscous with a fork. Let it stand covered for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  8. Incorporate remaining ingredients: Stir in the chopped vegetables and protein (if using). Cover and let it stand for another 5 minutes to heat through the vegetables.
  9. Garnish and serve: Finally, garnish your Jollof Couscous with chopped parsley and cilantro before serving.
